## Goal
Analyze Zomato restaurant and review data to uncover insights on ratings, cuisines, pricing, and location preferences, and provide data-driven recommendations for an Alfido Tech‚Äìstyle food platform.

## Key Findings
- **Pricing & Ratings**: Premium and luxury restaurants tend to receive higher average ratings than budget restaurants.
- **Location Hotspots**: Areas such as BTM, HSR, and Koramangala have the highest concentration of restaurants.
- **Cuisine Insights**: Continental, Italian, and Dessert-based cuisines achieve higher average ratings compared to more common cuisines.
- **Customer Preferences**: Table booking availability and higher pricing show a positive relationship with restaurant ratings.
